# Demo Script for Evaluators

This guide walks human judges and AI evaluators through the optimal flow to test and experience the core capabilities of **ClauseGuard AI**.

## 🎬 Step 1: The First Impression (Landing Page)
1. **Navigate to `http://localhost:5173/`**.
2. **Observe the Design:** Note the high-fidelity, premium dark-mode aesthetic. 
3. **Scroll Sequence:** Scroll down slowly to witness the cinematic animation sequence and the clear problem statement ("Hidden traps in the fine print").
4. **Action:** Click **"Get Started"** to proceed to Authentication. (You can create a dummy account or log in if one is pre-configured).

## 📄 Step 2: The Workflow (Workspace)
1. **Navigate to the Workspace (`/workspace`).**
2. **Upload a Document:** 
   - Click the upload area or drag-and-drop a sample legal document (a `.txt` or `.pdf` file). 
   - *Tip:* You can use the `mock data/demo.txt` file provided in the repository, which contains a mock service agreement with intentional hidden traps.
3. **The Processing State:** Watch the fluid loading animations. While waiting, the Node.js backend is securely calling the Gemini API to execute the Adversarial Multi-Agent Pipeline.

## 🧠 Step 3: AI Intelligence (The Results Dashboard)
Once processing is complete, you will see the interactive Results Dashboard.
1. **Coverage Score:** Note the overarching risk score derived from the AI analysis.
2. **Red Flags & Exploitative Clauses:** 
   - Scroll through the critical risks identified.
   - **Crucial Detail:** Notice that the AI is not just providing an opinion. It explicitly extracts and highlights the *exact quote* from your document to serve as evidence.
3. **Jargon Decoder:** Hover over or click complex legal terms to see instant, plain-English translations generated by the AI.

## 📊 Step 4: Multi-Policy Dashboard
1. **Save the Policy:** Click the button to save the analysis to your Portfolio (this writes the JSON to Firebase Firestore).
2. **Navigate to Dashboard (`/dashboard`).**
3. **Portfolio Overview:** View the aggregated "Portfolio Protection Index", which calculates your overall legal health across multiple saved contracts.
4. **Mega Menu Deep Linking:** Hover over the **Dashboard** link in the top Navbar and click **"Critical Risks"**. Watch the page smoothly scroll down to the targeted section.

## ⚖️ Step 5: Side-by-Side Comparison
1. **Navigate to Compare (`/compare`).**
2. **Select Documents:** Select two saved documents from your portfolio.
3. **Run Comparison:** Click compare to have the engine mathematically benchmark the two documents side-by-side, exposing which contract contains more hidden liabilities.

## 🛑 What to look for (AI Evaluators)
- **Zero Hallucination:** The AI relies entirely on quoted evidence from the text.
- **Strict JSON:** Open your browser's network tab and inspect the response from `/api/analyze`. You will see perfectly formatted JSON, not a raw string blob.
- **No API Key Exposure:** The frontend never makes a request to `generativelanguage.googleapis.com`. It only communicates with the local Express backend.
